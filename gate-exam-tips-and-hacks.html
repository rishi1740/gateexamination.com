    Gate Exam Tips And Hacks

Buy some small size note books or A4 size (50 pages or so) and keep one for each topic.

If you are bored then you aren't learning and you need to change something
Read two different subjects in parallel so that you wont feel bored .
Tough subject and one easy subject could be prepared each day . You can choose 2 light subjects too with a tough one and make proper notes.

Collect formulae on blank paper & paste them on the walls.

Prepare weekly plan to cover a particular topic syllabus set daily goals.

After reading a chapter solve some problems (at least 50 mcqs and some long problems). Mark problems you could not solve in first attempt.

Draw mind maps for some important topics if possible, it works.

Print out the syllabus in hard copy.

Mathematics (Discrete+Engg.) and Aptitude – 30 marks approx

Revise flashcards of all subject as many times possible.

Solve all previous year aptitude tests papers from all branches

Do one topic of aptitude daily
General Aptitude – 5 questions daily.

GATE requires continues the practice to score good marks. You must practice a lot for GATE exam and try to increase you speed of solving the question. Give time in solving a variety of different problems rather than practices similar type of questions. Go through mock tests when you complete the course as much as you can to grab your understanding and level.

Since MATHEMATICS + QUANTITATIVE APTITUDE holds 30 marks and they are huge subjects to cover, it is advised that one should give at least 30 minutes each everyday. This subjects are more on practice and less to read/study.

First and foremost, visualise yourself as cracking GATE. By this, we mean have the determination and the confidence that you will clear GATE with excellent scores. This belief is going to play a major role in helping you succeed.

Go through the latest syllabus of GATE thoroughly and make a list of subjects that you need to concentrate. Keep hard copy of syllabus with you.

Many of GATE questions can be answered with reading and understanding So final goal is to improve your time to solve each question.

GATEoverflow is adding Full length tests in previous year Papers, I strongly advice you that keep last 3-4 years GATE questions (Say from GATE 14-17) untouched and use them as mock exams along with this test series. This real Exams will be much more effective.

We can say that GATE paper is much predictive in a way it more leaned toward a particular topic.So first task to do is go through all the papers (I think the organizing institute 2019 may be Madras and prev years when madras was organizing institute were 1991, 1997, 2003…) and find out the similarities ,Stress on them.

During GATE exam Finish the exam early and identified silly mistakes in remaining 20 minutes which saves marks.

For tricky problems related with programming visit department websites of various foreign universities and solve the assignments given there.

Do Reverse Engineering try to get the correct answers from the answers itself and work all the way up to questions.
